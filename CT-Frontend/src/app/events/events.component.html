ICoAC
Conference

ICoAC is an international conference in the field of Computer Science and Communication, focusing to address issues and developments in advanced computing. This conference seeks to bring together international researchers to present papers and generate discussions in recent trends and developments of computing. It is organized by the Department of Computer Technology annually during December. Know more about ICoAC in official website.
